The Revolution Starts Here

Stand With Savail

Four terms max. No lobbying—when I step down, I’m done. I won’t turn around and work for the same corporate or political interests I fought against. No cozying up to party bosses. I’ll fight for housing, healthcare, and fair wages, then step aside for the next generation. Too many in Congress treat their seats like lifetime appointments, turning the place into something resembling both a country club and a nursing home, when it should be the beating heart of America’s future. Our survival as a democracy is at stake. If we don’t break the grip of career politicians and the special interests that own them, we risk losing the very foundation of our republic. Stand with me to open the doors of Congress to the next generation and build a government that works for the people, not the powerful.
